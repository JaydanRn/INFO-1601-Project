General Questions

D'Spot is a community-driven food spot locator app that helps you discover and share great food places. Users can upload locations, descriptions, ratings, and view them on an interactive map.

Yes! D'Spot is completely free to use for both discovering and contributing food spots.

Adding & Managing Spots

Click on "Create Spot" in the navigation, fill in the details (name, location, description, etc.), and submit. Your spot will be visible to other users after approval.

Yes, you can edit or delete any spots you've contributed. Find your spot and look for the edit/delete options.

Map Features

Click on any spot in the carousel or list view to see its location displayed on the embedded Google Map.

Yes! Click on the map placeholder for any spot, then click the Google Maps logo in the bottom corner to open full navigation options.

User Features

When viewing a spot, click the heart icon or "Add to Favorites" button. You can access all your favorites from the Favorites section.

Absolutely! Open any spot's details to see the comments section where you can share your experiences and read others' reviews.

Ratings are on a 1-5 scale. The average of all user ratings is displayed for each spot. You can rate a spot after visiting it.
